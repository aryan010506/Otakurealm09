CH. 4 • TOP LISTS

THE BIGGEST
DEBATES

Villains, plot twists, openings, and hidden gems — all ranked.

#01
PLOT TWISTS

TOP 10 ANIME PLOT TWISTS

  1. Attack on Titan — The Basement Reveal. It recontextualized everything.
  2. Death Note — L Dies. The hero lost. Unprecedented.
  3. Code Geass — Zero Requiem. The ultimate sacrifice play.
  4. Naruto — Itachi's Truth. The massacre explained.
  5. Madoka Magica — Episode 3. Created the dark magical girl subgenre.
  6. Steins;Gate — Mayuri's Death Loop. Hits like a freight train.
  7. One Piece — Ace's Death. Oda broke his own rules.
  8. Fullmetal Alchemist — Nina Tucker. "Ed...ward."
  9. Evangelion — The Instrumentality. The show deconstructs itself.
  10. The Promised Neverland — The Farm Reveal. From warm to horrific in 30 seconds.
#02
VILLAINS

TOP 10 ANIME VILLAINS

  1. Johan Liebert (Monster) — The most terrifyingly human villain.
  2. Griffith (Berserk) — Griffith did EVERYTHING wrong.
  3. Light Yagami (Death Note) — The protagonist IS the villain.
  4. Meruem (Hunter x Hunter) — Began as a monster, ended as the most empathetic.
  5. Aizen Sosuke (Bleach) — Planned everything for centuries.
  6. Pain/Nagato (Naruto) — Made Naruto question his entire philosophy.
  7. DIO (JoJo's Bizarre Adventure) — The most iconic. "It was me, DIO!"
  8. Frieza (Dragon Ball Z) — Set the template for every shonen villain.
  9. Yoshikage Kira (JoJo Part 4) — A serial killer who wants a quiet life.
  10. Sukuna (Jujutsu Kaisen) — The King of Curses treats everyone as insects.
#03
MUSIC

TOP 10 ANIME OPENING THEMES (OP)

  1. "Cruel Angel's Thesis" (Evangelion) — The undisputed king of anime OPs.
  2. "Unravel" (Tokyo Ghoul) — Iconic angst.
  3. "Tank!" (Cowboy Bebop) — Flawless jazz masterpiece.
  4. "Again" (Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood) — YUI delivered perfection.
  5. "Gurenge" (Demon Slayer) — Lisa's breakout global hit.
  6. "The Rumbling" (Attack on Titan) — SiM brought the heavy metal hype.
  7. "Kaikai Kitan" (Jujutsu Kaisen) — Eve defined the modern shonen sound.
  8. "Idol" (Oshi no Ko) — YOASOBI broke global streaming records.
  9. "Colors" (Code Geass) — JIBUN WO!
  10. "Bloody Stream" (JoJo Part 2) — The most stylish OP ever made.
#04
ACTION

TOP 10 FIGHT SCENES

  1. Kakashi vs. Obito (Naruto Shippuden) — Flawless hand-to-hand choreography.
  2. Netero vs. Meruem (Hunter x Hunter) — A philosophical battle of ideals.
  3. Saitama vs. Boros (One Punch Man) — Pure, unadulterated animation hype.
  4. Gojo vs. Sukuna (Jujutsu Kaisen) — The absolute peak of modern shonen battles.
  5. Levi vs. Beast Titan (Attack on Titan) — The most satisfying beatdown in history.
  6. Tengen vs. Gyutaro (Demon Slayer) — Ufotable broke the internet.
  7. Goku vs. Jiren (Dragon Ball Super) — Broke streaming services globally.
  8. Gintoki vs. Takasugi (Gintama) — Pure emotion and brutal realism.
  9. Kiritsugu vs. Kirei (Fate/Zero) — A masterclass in tactical combat.
  10. Mob vs. Koyama (Mob Psycho 100) — Yutaka Nakamura's finest animation work.
#05
WORLD BUILDING

TOP 10 POWER SYSTEMS

  1. Nen (Hunter x Hunter) — The most balanced, deep, and logical system ever created.
  2. Alchemy (Fullmetal Alchemist) — Equivalent exchange makes it grounded and fair.
  3. Stands (JoJo's Bizarre Adventure) — Invented the "unique ghost power" trope.
  4. Cursed Energy / Domain Expansions (Jujutsu Kaisen) — Complex, lethal, and visually stunning.
  5. Chakra (Naruto) — The perfect blend of martial arts and elemental magic (before the alien stuff).
  6. Devil Fruits & Haki (One Piece) — Creative, bizarre, and balances perfectly with Haki later on.
  7. Triggers (World Trigger) — Highly tactical, team-based weapon system.
  8. Bending (Avatar) — Culturally tied to martial arts forms (yes, it counts).
  9. Zanpakuto (Bleach) — Shikai and Bankai reveals are pure hype.
  10. Quirks (My Hero Academia) — A fun, genetic take on western superhero powers.
#06
TRAGEDY

TOP 10 SADDEST ANIME

  1. Grave of the Fireflies — You will only watch this once. It is too painful.
  2. Clannad: After Story — The gold standard for tearing out your heart.
  3. Your Lie in April — A beautiful, inevitable tragedy.
  4. Anohana: The Flower We Saw That Day — "You found me."
  5. Violet Evergarden — Specifically Episode 10. Bring tissues.
  6. A Silent Voice — A brutal look at bullying and self-hatred.
  7. Cyberpunk: Edgerunners — High-octane action ending in hollow devastation.
  8. Plastic Memories — You know the ending from episode 1, but it still hurts.
  9. I Want to Eat Your Pancreas — Don't let the title fool you.
  10. To Your Eternity — The pain of outliving everyone you love.
#07
GENRE

TOP 10 ISEKAI ANIME

  1. Re:Zero — A psychological breakdown of the isekai power fantasy.
  2. Mushoku Tensei — The godfather of the modern isekai web novel.
  3. KonoSuba — The funniest parody of the genre.
  4. No Game No Life — Visually stunning mind games.
  5. That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime — The best world/nation building.
  6. Overlord — What if the main character was the final boss?
  7. Sword Art Online — The show that started the modern boom.
  8. Log Horizon — For players who actually care about MMO politics and economics.
  9. Spirited Away — The Academy Award-winning classic isekai.
  10. The Rising of the Shield Hero (Season 1) — An excellent underdog revenge story.
