#01
2025 HIT

SOLO LEVELING

★★★★★ 9.2

Solo Leveling finally received its anime adaptation in 2024, and A-1 Pictures delivered. Sung Jinwoo's transformation from the weakest hunter to an unstoppable shadow monarch is pure adrenaline.

What elevates Solo Leveling beyond standard power fantasy is the atmosphere. The dungeons feel genuinely threatening, the monster designs are creative, and the leveling system creates addictive progression.

VERDICT: If you love power fantasies, Solo Leveling is the best the genre has to offer.

#02
MASTERPIECE

FRIEREN: BEYOND JOURNEY'S END

★★★★★ 9.7

Frieren is unlike any fantasy anime you've ever seen. The first episode's funeral scene is one of the most emotionally devastating openings in anime history.

VERDICT: This is a once-in-a-decade anime. An instant classic.
